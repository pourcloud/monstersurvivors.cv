Game Features

  • • Endless survival gameplay
  • • Multiple character choices
  • • Unique power-ups and abilities

How to Play

  • • Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • • Collect experience orbs to level up

  • • Survive as long as possible!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game that puts your skills to the ultimate test. Face waves of increasingly difficult monsters while collecting power-ups and experience to become stronger. With its roguelike elements and permanent progression system, each run offers a unique experience.

Perfect your strategy, unlock new characters, and discover powerful combinations of abilities as you aim to survive longer and achieve higher scores. The game features beautiful pixel art graphics, smooth controls, and an engaging progression system that will keep you coming back for more.
